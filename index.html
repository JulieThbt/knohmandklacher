It’s what’s in a name.


So what’s in a name anyway? After all, wouldn’t a rose by any other name … Well, you know the rest.

What all of us can take away from this well-known quote (as a certain well-known and equally well-enamored sixteenth-century Veronese teenager can attest) is that whether your name is Montague or Capulet can really put a bit of a monkey wrench in your plans and end up, well, in tragedy. Words matter. And finding just the right words can often be tricky. That’s why you leave that work to us.

At Knohm & Klacher, our wordsmiths know how to craft just the right name out of just the right words. For us, it's both a science and an art. But mostly a passion. We’ll take care of the entire lengthy process for you. From making sure you avoid legal trouble in case your great name idea is already taken, to scouring all possible meanings and avoiding associations you don’t want, as well as deciding how to say and spell the name once we've matched you with the right one. You create it, we name it.
